About Me

Hi, I'm Dr. Tin Min Htoo, Deputy Director of the Pollution Control Division at the Environmental Conservation Department, Ministry of Natural Resources and Environmental Conservation, Myanmar. With a Ph.D. in Environmental Studies, I have dedicated my career to studying and implementing solutions to pollution and environmental conservation challenges. I am passionate about sharing knowledge and driving positive change to protect the environment.

Projects

Over the years, I have been involved in several impactful projects aimed at reducing pollution and promoting sustainable environmental practices. Below are some of the key projects I've worked on:

  • Water Quality Improvement Initiative (WQII) – Implemented sustainable water treatment solutions to reduce industrial pollutants in rivers.
  • Air Quality Management Program (AQMP) – Developed strategies for monitoring and reducing air pollution in urban areas.
  • Plastic Waste Reduction Campaign – Led a national awareness campaign to reduce single-use plastics in Myanmar.
